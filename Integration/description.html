Overview

Deploy Datadog agent(s) directly to Google Container Engine to:

  • Visualize your GCP Cluster performance in real time
  • Collect performance metrics for your containers, pods and overall infrastructure
  • Get realtime alerts on the status of your kubelets
You can also send custom metrics to Datadog.
