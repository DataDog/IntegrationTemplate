Opsmatic

Opsmatic provides real-time visibility of any change in the live state of computing infrastructure and intelligently alerts users before trouble begins.

Overview

Connect Opsmatic to Datadog to get:

  • Instant awareness of any critical change
  • Full visibility of the live state and history of all your hosts

Configuration

To see Opsmatic events in your DataDog stream:

  • Add your DataDog API key to your Opsmatic Integrations page.
  • Configure your Opsmatic notifications to go to DataDog.
  • Check out our DataDog Integration blog post for more information.
